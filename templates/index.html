SITAR

Have you ever really liked a playlist but bored of listening to same songs over and over? Well with the Spotify Integrated Tailored Audio Recommendations, or SITAR, you are able to generate a playlist of songs that are similar to the overall vibe of any of your previously created playlists!

How Does SITAR Work?

Spotify assigns different attributes to each song in their catalog. SITAR uses a handful of the most important tracked attributes and group them up with other songs from you playlist with similar attributes. These songs are then used as a basis for recommendation and the process is repeated the amount of tracks in a given playlist.
